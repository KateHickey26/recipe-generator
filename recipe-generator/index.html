Recipe Title

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tomato
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Chop the tomato and cheese.
  2. Whisk the eggs and add the chopped ingredients.
  3. Cook in a pan for 5–7 minutes until set.
